# Installation Guide

## Step 1

Updated the Kali Linux package repository.

## Step 2

Installed Apache2 and verified that the service was running.

## Step 3

Installed PHP together with all required extensions for DVWA.

## Step 4

Installed and started the MariaDB database server.

## Step 5

Downloaded DVWA into the Apache web root.

## Step 6

Configured the DVWA configuration file.

## Step 7

Created the MariaDB database required by DVWA.

## Step 8

Restarted Apache.

## Step 9

Opened DVWA in a browser.

## Step 10

Completed the initial database setup through the DVWA web interface.

## Step 11

Logged into DVWA and selected the Low security level for the lab exercise.
